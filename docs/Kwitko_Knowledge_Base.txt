## BREWING GUIDES
- Espresso: 18-20g in, ~36g out, 25-30s, water ~93C. Use a fine grind and a clean portafilter.
- Pour-Over (V60/dripper): 1:16 ratio (e.g. 20g coffee : 320g water), medium grind, 2:30-3:00 total, bloom 30s.
- French Press: 1:15 ratio, coarse grind, 4 minute steep, press slowly.
- AeroPress: 15g coffee, 200g water at 85C, steep 1:30, press 30s.
- Storage: keep beans in an airtight container away from light, heat and moisture; use within 3-4 weeks of roast for peak flavor.
